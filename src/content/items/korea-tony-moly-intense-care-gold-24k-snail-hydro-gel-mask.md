Two-piece hydrogel sheet pressed with fermented snail mucin and 24K gold. The gold sounds theatrical; it's actually a delivery mechanism for the snail filtrate, which is the real workhorse for elasticity and radiance. Tony Moly has been making K-beauty since 2006 — Micki buys these to convert the snail-skeptical and as the kind of stocking stuffer that gets noticed. The 24K Gold mask comes and goes online; if it's sold out, Tony Moly's everyday [Snail Secretion mask](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0085RKPUU?tag=trovecollec0d-20) is the always-in-stock sibling — same snail workhorse, a few dollars a sheet. — Eris
