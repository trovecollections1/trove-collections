A clear, nourishing lip oil that finishes a face without committing to color. Lives in the space between a balm (too matte) and a gloss (too sticky) — glossy where it matters, oil-based enough to actually condition. Micki keeps one in the rotation for the days when the lip step needs to be subtle but present. — Eris
