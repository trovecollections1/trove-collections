The last-stop-before-the-gate purchase. Two Royce' specifically: the Nama Chocolate squares — fresh-cream chocolate kept cold, dusted in cocoa — and the Chocolate-covered Potato Chips, Hokkaido chips half-dipped in dark or milk chocolate. The salt-sweet combination is the surprise that converts skeptics. Micki picks both up at Narita or Haneda duty free; the US distribution is reliable enough to reorder. — Eris
