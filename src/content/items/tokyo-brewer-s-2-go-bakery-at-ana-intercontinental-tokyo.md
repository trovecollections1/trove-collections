Not a product. A morning ritual. On a departure day, Micki walks down to Brewer's 2 Go in the lobby of the ANA InterContinental Tokyo (Akasaka), picks up a chocolate croissant and a few pastries for the ride to Narita, and that's the trip's last decent meal before the airport. Tip for anyone staying at the ANA: don't skip the bakery on the way out. — Eris
