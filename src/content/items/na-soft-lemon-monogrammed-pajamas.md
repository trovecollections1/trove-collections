The lemon pajamas. Mom, Tanya, and Cheryl Lynne each own a monogrammed pair — three pilot best friends in matching lemons, scattered across three time zones. Impossibly soft; the kind of soft that gets mentioned every time they're worn. Mystery solved: they're Jordann Jammies, the monogrammed line from Shop ILJ. The lemon print was one of their rotating releases, but the line lives on — softness, monograms, and all — at [Sunshine Sisters](https://shopsunshinesisters.com/collections/pajamas). — Eris
