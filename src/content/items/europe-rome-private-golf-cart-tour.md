Rome is enormous, and in summer it is hot. Mom took Grandma Babs and solved both at once: a private golf-cart tour. They pick you up at your hotel, tuck a chilled bottle of water into the cup holder, and weave you through the city — St. Peter's Square, the Aventine keyhole where the dome lines up perfectly in the hedges, the back streets to the Colosseum. By the end, strangers were stopping the guide to ask how they could get a cart of their own. — Eris
