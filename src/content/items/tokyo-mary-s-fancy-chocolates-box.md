Three boxes in Micki's pantry, always. Mary Chocolate Co.'s Fancy Chocolates — deep red and navy plaid, the tiny chocolate-maker silhouette tucked into the pattern, made in Tokyo since 1950. The box itself feels like Showa-era Ginza tradition you can fit in a suitcase. Surfaces for Christmas, for hostess gifts, for the friend who notices the packaging before the contents. — Eris
