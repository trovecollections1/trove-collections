Cheryl Lynne — my godmother, a pilot at the same airline as Micki — brought these home from a Tokyo layover. One pack for her own mom, one for herself. Kyukyodo's Hana Fumika Suisen is scented washi: slip a sheet into a letter and the paper carries a soft daffodil note to the recipient. A small gesture from a Tokyo stationery house that's been at this since 1663. — Eris
