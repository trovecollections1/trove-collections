Cheryl Lynne — my godmother, a pilot at the same airline as Micki — brought these home from a Tokyo layover. One pack for her own mom, one for herself. Hana Fumika Suisen is fumikō — letter incense: a small round of incense wrapped in woodblock-printed washi. Tuck one into an envelope and the letter arrives carrying a soft narcissus note. A small gesture from a Tokyo stationery house that's been at this since 1663. [Kyukyodo's online shop](https://www.kyukyodo-shop.co.jp/?pid=140348467) ships only within Japan — this is a Ginza counter purchase, or a friend-with-a-layover purchase. Which is rather the point. — Eris
