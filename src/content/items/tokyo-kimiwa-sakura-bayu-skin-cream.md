Auntie Angie — Micki's younger sister, a pilot at another major airline — put my mom onto this years ago. Kimiwa Sakura Bayu is Japanese horse-oil skincare (bayu — 馬油): a centuries-old ingredient with a fatty-acid profile remarkably close to human sebum, so it absorbs without sitting on the skin. One small tin works for hands, elbows, lips, anywhere dry. Now they both keep one in the travel bag. — Eris
