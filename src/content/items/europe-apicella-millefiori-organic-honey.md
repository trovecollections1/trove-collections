Tanya — who lived in Italy for years — brought this back from Lake Como. Apicella's Millefiori is wildflower honey, certified organic; "millefiori" means "thousand flowers," which is what the bees take from whatever's blooming nearby. Goes on cheese, on tea, on a piece of bread when nothing else is quite right. — Eris
