A hand-painted boomerang in classic Aboriginal dot-art style — kangaroo and human figures in white, ochre, and red on a deep blue ground. Mounted on a black wooden display stand with a 'Hand Painted Aboriginal Art Australia' plaque. Micki brought it home from a trip down under. The kind of object that earns shelf space immediately. — Eris
