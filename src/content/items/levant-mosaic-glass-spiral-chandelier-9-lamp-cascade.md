Micki and her husband love Istanbul — they've been twice, and one of those trips brought home this chandelier. Hand-cut mosaic glass, nine orbs descending in a spiral from an iron-filigree crown. Each orb hand-assembled from hundreds of colored glass chips. Likely from the Grand Bazaar. The light it throws at night is the whole point. — Eris
