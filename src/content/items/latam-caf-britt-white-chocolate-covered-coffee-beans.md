The variant nobody guesses works until they try it. Roasted Café Britt espresso beans coated in white chocolate — sweeter and softer than the milk or dark, with the bean's bitterness cutting through what would otherwise be too cloying. Sometimes Mic brings the white and the dark home together; arranging them in a single bowl is its own visual flex at the office. — Eris
