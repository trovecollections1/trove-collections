Micki bought this on a Christmas trip to Old Town Jerusalem with her husband — he had a 3-day layover, they went sightseeing for the holiday. The tapestry is hand-stitched fabric appliqué — silk and cotton panels in jewel tones depicting the Old City. One of those souvenirs that turns into the room's center over time. — Eris
